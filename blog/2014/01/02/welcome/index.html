Welcome

Jan 2nd, 2014

Welcome to my development blog. I am Harley and I will be your host. I am a junior developer looking to make my way into the field full-time. I have an A.A.S. in Computer Information Technology, and started to work on a B.S. in CS. I am not currently attending school officially; but that doesn’t mean that I’m not still studying and learning. I have worked with C++ and PHP in the past, but am currently working with Ruby. I have one Rails project that I’ve started called Bacon Splitter. Feel free to check it out and provide feedback if you have any. I’m also working my way through the book “Rails 4 in Action” from Manning, which has been a tremendous resource for me.

I have wanted to start a dev blog for some time now, but the main impetus for me starting this one now, comes from a few places. I’ve been inspired by Joshua Kemp and his story of going from no degree to a junior developer in less than a year. Avdi Grimm has mentioned that he likes to read developer blogs, so I hoped to give him one more source to read. Also, Mark Sim, one of my first remote pairing partners, led me to Octopress as the framework to use. There are certainly other people who have both inspired me, and helped me learn; and I hope to give them the proper credit on this blog as we move forward.

I can be found on Github and Twitter. Feel free to hit me up either place.
