Rubygems Certificate Verify Failed

Feb 11th, 2014

When trying to bundle install on one of my Rails apps, I got an error saying that ‘certificate verify failed’. I believe the rvm certificates were out of date. What I found to work was rvm rubygems latest. After that I did my bin/bundle and everything worked perfectly.
